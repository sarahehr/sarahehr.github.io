About Me


My name is Sarah Ehrlich. I'm a journalism and agricultural education student at Colorado State. Born and raised in Colorado, I am currently residing in Fort Collins, CO, but I call the San Luis Valley of Southern Colorado my first home.

My first interest in photography came to the surface after taking a digital photography class in high school, and after my parents bought the family a Canon Rebel T3i. I adopted the camera and brought it with me to college, and she continues to be my main weapon of choice, until I can afford new lenses, cameras, etc.

This portfolio also includes some writing samples from my time as an arts and culture reporter at the Rocky Mountain Collegian. I will graduate in the fall of 2018 with a degree in journalism with a minor in agricultural education. I hope my future career endeavors include education and outreach opportunities about agriculture, science and the arts.
